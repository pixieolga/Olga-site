Cinematic masterpieces worth watching

What is a cinematic masterpiece you might wonder? A cinematic masterpiece is a film that is widely regarded as outstanding in terms of its artistic, technical, and storytelling achievements. It is a subjective designation often given to movies that have made a significant impact on the history of cinema, received critical acclaim, won prestigious awards, and/or influenced other filmmakers and films.
